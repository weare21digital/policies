Privacy Policy

Royal Portrait

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

Royal Portrait ("the App") is a photo art application that transforms your photos into royal-style portraits using AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

  • Photos: When you use the App to create a portrait, your selected photo is sent to our servers for AI processing. Photos are processed ephemerally and are not stored permanently on our servers. They are deleted after processing is complete.
  • In-App Purchases: The App offers in-app coin packs. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.).
  • No accounts: The App does not require or create any user accounts.
  • No analytics: The App does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • No advertising ID: The App does not use Android's advertising ID.

Network Access

The App requires internet access to send photos to our AI processing servers and to receive the generated portrait results. No data is sent to any third-party advertising or analytics services.

Data Storage

Generated portraits and app preferences are stored locally on your device. Photos submitted for processing are transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption and are deleted from our servers after processing. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing: Used to process in-app purchases. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data during processing.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
